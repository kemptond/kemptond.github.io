I'm an Assistant Professor here at Carleton. My research interests are applied and computational math, differential equations, and symmetry.

I came to Carleton in 2015 after a postdoc at Harvey Mudd College in Claremont, CA. Before that I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Macalester College in Saint Paul. I did my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the University of Minnesota, supervised by Peter Olver.
